Timeline & Cash Deployment

What to expect, month by month.

A standard 223(f) closes in five to six months from engagement. Most of the borrower cash is back-loaded — the early months are primarily third-party report costs.

Process Timeline
Mo. 1
Engagement & Kickoff
Lender engaged. Order PCNA, Phase I/HEROS, appraisal, ALTA survey. Borrower begins due diligence.
Mo. 2
Draft Reports & Due Diligence
First-draft third-party reports return. Lender reconciles inconsistencies before they reach HUD.
Mo. 3
Firm Application Submission
Reports finalize, loan committee approves, full Firm Application submitted to HUD with the 0.30% application fee.
Mo. 4
HUD Review
HUD underwrites the Firm Application. A clean, internally consistent package moves through quickly.
Mo. 5
Firm Commitment & Rate Lock
HUD issues Firm Commitment. Rate lock with 0.50% good faith deposit (credited at closing). Closing process begins.
Mo. 6
Final Closing & Fund
Closing package finalized, documents executed, loan funds.
